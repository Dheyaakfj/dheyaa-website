About Us

The Iraq Financial Observatory (IFO) is an independent, non-governmental organization established to promote transparency and understanding of Iraq's public finances. We are a team of economists, researchers, and policy analysts dedicated to providing data-driven insights and objective analysis of the Iraqi economy. Our mission is to empower citizens, journalists, and policymakers with the information they need to engage in constructive dialogue and hold public institutions accountable. We believe that a well-informed public is essential for good governance and sustainable economic development.

Our Work

We provide independent analysis, monitor regulatory changes, and produce economic insights to foster transparency and accountability in Iraq.

Analysis
